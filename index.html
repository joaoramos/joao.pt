Digital product design as an hollistic practice

My take on product design and how I conduct my work

I believe thoughtful product design addresses people, context, and problem as a whole, not as isolated elements. I usually take them in by leveraging user research and data insights early on when kicking off a project.

Most of my time is spent asking questions, scribbling diagrams and wireframes, drawing testicles on post-its; often also co-designing and co-programming with other designers and enginners.
